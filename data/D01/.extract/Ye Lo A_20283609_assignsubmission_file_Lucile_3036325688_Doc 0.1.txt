Title
Understanding the interaction between social anxiety and appearance anxiety: Pathways and
impacts on well-being

Research question
How do social anxiety and appearance anxiety interact and reinforce each other? By
understanding this relationship, what design and intervention opportunities can be created to
support well-being and social sustainability?

Literature review
There are three studies all showing the effect of appearance anxiety to social anxiety. The
cross-sectional study states that individuals with appearance anxiety have higher risk of social
anxiety, but self-compassion can buffer against this relationship (Gao et al., 2023). The
experimental and longitudinal studies show the appearance of concern in females can lead to
social anxiety and depression (Patel and Cougle, 2024a, Patel and Cougle, 2025, Wilver et al.,
2020).
